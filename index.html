San Francisco Dispatches

With an increase in population comes a surge of incoming emergency calls. These calls can be analyzed, helping city officials better prepare for an emergency situation.

Under the Battalions tab, I have created some visuals to describe the data based on the ten battalions, or emergency response districts, of San Francisco.

In the map section, I created a heatmap of the given 10,000 dispatches to identify the hot spots of emergency calls, which can be sorted based on type.

Finally, under the Disptach Predictor tab, enter a time and address to predict what type of emergency dispatch would be required.
